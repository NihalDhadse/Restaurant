COVID-19 Safety Protocol

1. Hygiene & Sanitization

  • All surfaces are sanitized every 2 hours.
  • Hand sanitizers are available at entrances and tables.
  • Tables and menus are disinfected after every use.

2. Staff Guidelines

  • All staff are temperature-checked daily.
  • Face masks and gloves are mandatory for all kitchen
    and service staff.
  • Regular health and hygiene training is provided.

3. Social Distancing & Seating

  • Tables are spaced to maintain a 6-foot distance.
  • Contactless ordering and digital menus are encouraged.
  • Maximum occupancy limits are strictly followed.

4. Delivery & Takeaway

  • Zero-contact delivery is available on request.
  • All packaging is sanitized before dispatch.
  • Delivery staff wear protective gear at all times.

5. Guest Guidelines

  • Guests are requested to wear masks when not eating.
  • Temperature screening is conducted at entry.
  • Use sanitizer stations before entering and after leaving.
We appreciate your cooperation and patience. These protocols are in place to ensure the safety of all our customers and staff.
